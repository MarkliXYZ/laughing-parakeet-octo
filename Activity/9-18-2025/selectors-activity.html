About

Why selectors?

Selectors let you reach into the DOM and style the right elements. Specificity matters: ID > class > element.

  • “Selectors are like superpowers.”
  • “Use the right tool for the job.”
  • “Keep it accessible and inclusive!”

Types of selectors cheatsheet

  • Element/type: p, h1
  • Class: .card, .btn
  • ID: #welcome, #shop
  • Pseudo-class: :first-child, :hover
Note: You’ll style only direct child headings in a challenge using .card > h3.
